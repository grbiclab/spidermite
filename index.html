Welcome to the Grbić Lab

We are the Spider Mite Team at Western University in Ontario, Canada. Our research focuses on the genomics and genetics of spider mite–host plant interactions, adaptation, and resistance to pesticides.

Services

We provide services to identify spider mite species and assess their resistance status using molecular and phenotypic assays.
