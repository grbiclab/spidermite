Welcome to the Grbić Lab

We are the Spider Mite Team at Western University in Ontario, Canada. Our research focuses on the genomics and genetics of spider mite–host plant interactions, adaptation, and resistance to pesticides. Our goal is to understand the molecular and evolutionary mechanisms driving pest adaptation and to support sustainable agriculture through genomic insights.

Services

We provide services to identify spider mite species (Acaros) and assess their resistance status. Our team uses molecular and phenotypic assays to determine species composition and resistance to common pesticides.

Sample Submission Instructions

If you wish to send plant samples infested with spider mites for identification and resistance assessment, please follow these steps:

  1. Collect a few leaves showing active spider mite infestation.
  2. Place the samples in a small, ventilated container or paper envelope (avoid plastic bags if possible).
  3. Include details about the host plant, location, date, and observed pesticide treatments.
  4. Mail the samples to our lab at the address below, labeled “Spider Mite Sample – Grbić Lab”.

Contact us beforehand at grbiclab@uwo.ca for submission approval and guidance.
